  • Am I getting value for money with Ikoniq Construction?
  1. Typically, the cost of construction will range from 20,000 to 40,000 on a per square basis excluding perimeter walls, major lighting fixtures (ie. Chandelier, droplights etc.) and landscaping. This is a very high level estimate and shall only be used for reference. Factors that may influence cost are the following:
    1. Type of Finish and Design Intricacy: Naturally, industrial or raw type of finishes would cost less than more specific design styles with intricate details and materials.
    2. Timeline: Working on a project with a tighter timeline would require additional manpower and longer working hours.
    3. Geography: A lot lower than the finished floor line will require landfill or “panambak.”
  2. Ideally, a residential project can be completed in 6-8 months. Factors that may affect timeline are the following:
    1. Floor Area
    2. Type of Finish and Design Intricacy
